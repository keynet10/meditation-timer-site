Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Meditation Bell Timer mobile application (the "App") and the website meditationbelltimer.com (the "Site").

We believe that your meditation practice is entirely your own business. We have built this project with a strict privacy-first approach.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No Accounts: No sign-up is required for the App or the Site.
  • No Tracking: We do not track individual usage, session lengths, or your specific audio preferences.
  • Local Storage: In the App and the Web Timer, your settings are saved locally on your own device and are never sent to a server.

2. Website Analytics

To understand general traffic patterns (such as which articles are being read), our Site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics. This service is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or collect personal information. It provides us only with aggregate data like total page views and general geographic regions.

3. Advertising

Meditation Bell Timer is a premium, user-supported project. We do not host advertisements, and we do not sell your "attention" or data to third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

Any future updates will only be to reflect changes in legal requirements or new features. Our core philosophy of zero data collection will not change.
